Seven weeks of making, breaking, and learning to invent from scratch again, all documented here as I go.

Hi, I'm Gurmani/Guru!

I'm an Industrial and Product Design student at Pratt Institute, here at Harvard to learn digital fabrication and train in the technical facet of design. I love inventing things that matter and solving problems at the intersection of design, technology, and human experience. Let's change the world together, one project at a time!

This site documents every week of the course: the ideas, the failures, the builds, and the final project. If something here inspires you to make something yourself, even better. Just don't forget to share it with me!
